Welcome to Casa Cunera

Casa Cunera is a refined villa in the exclusive La Viña area of Benissa Costa, created for guests who value comfort, privacy and a beautiful Mediterranean setting. With generous outdoor living spaces, a private pool and uninterrupted sea views, the villa offers a relaxed and elevated stay for families and groups of friends.

Prime Location

Casa Cunera is located in the exclusive La Viña area of Benissa Costa, offering a peaceful setting with panoramic sea views and complete privacy.

The bay of Baladrar is just 3 kilometres away, while Moraira and Calpe are within easy reach for restaurants, beaches and water activities.

A supermarket is only a 5-minute drive away.

Alicante Airport: 95 km
Valencia Airport: 140 km

The Villa

The main house features a bright glass terrace with sea views, a spacious living and dining area, an open kitchen, two bedrooms with double beds and a stylish bathroom. The kitchen is fully equipped with a gas stove, oven, microwave, dishwasher and Nespresso coffee machine.

Independent Apartment

Accessible by stairs from the pool terrace, the independent apartment offers extra privacy and flexibility. It includes a living room with satellite TV, a fully equipped kitchen, one bedroom with two single beds, a trundle bed for two additional guests and a bathroom.
